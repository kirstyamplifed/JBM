Our Identity

Built by operators, for operators.

We aren't career recruiters reading from a script. We are former founders, scaleup executives, and startup advisors who know what great execution looks like.

JBM was founded on a simple premise: the traditional executive search model was broken for fast-moving technology companies. It was too slow, too corporate, and lacked an understanding of startup DNA.

We built a firm that moves at the speed of venture capital. A firm that values operational scars over polished resumes. We leverage a deeply personal, curated network to connect the right minds at the exact right moment of scale.
